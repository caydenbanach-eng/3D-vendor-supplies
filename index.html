REFUND POLICY

Last updated: June 2026

All products sold by 3D Vendor Supplies are custom-made to order. Because each item is produced specifically for you, we do not accept returns or issue refunds except in the circumstances described below.

Eligible Refund Situations

1. Item damaged in transit — If your order arrives visibly damaged, you may request a refund or replacement. You must contact us within 7 days of the delivery date and provide clear photographic evidence of the damage, including photos of the item and the original packaging.

2. Item lost in transit — If your package is confirmed lost by the carrier (tracking shows no movement for 15+ business days after the expected delivery date), you may request a refund or replacement at no additional charge.

How to Submit a Claim

Contact us through the contact form on this site within the applicable window. Include your order details and, for damage claims, attach clear photos as proof. We will respond within 3 business days. Approved refunds will be issued to your original payment method within 5–10 business days.

Non-Refundable Situations

We are unable to issue refunds for:

  • Change of mind or accidental orders
  • Incorrect shipping addresses provided by the customer
  • Carrier delays outside our control
  • Minor color variations due to screen display or filament batch differences
  • Claims submitted more than 7 days after delivery

Note

All items are made to order. Please review your order carefully before submitting. If you have questions before purchasing, reach out via our contact form — we're happy to help.

PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: June 2026

3D Vendor Supplies ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

  • Contact information — name and email address when you submit our contact or order form
  • Order information — items ordered and quantities
  • Payment information — processed securely by a third-party processor (e.g. Stripe). We do not store your card details
  • Shipping information — your mailing address, used solely to fulfill your order

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill your order
  • To communicate with you about your order or inquiry
  • To improve our products and customer experience

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We share data only with service providers necessary to operate our business (such as shipping carriers and payment processors), and only to the extent required to fulfill your order.

Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill your order and comply with applicable legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us through the form on this site.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please reach out via the contact form on this site.
